Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts in a local conference center. What started as a modest event has grown into one of the premier tech conferences worldwide, with over 10,000 attendees at our most recent event.

Over the years, we've hosted groundbreaking talks, launched innovative products, and created a community that spans continents. Our commitment to accessible technology education has remained constant throughout our growth.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is threefold:

  • To democratize access to cutting-edge technology knowledge
  • To foster meaningful connections between tech professionals
  • To showcase innovations that will shape our future

We believe technology should be inclusive, ethical, and beneficial to all humanity. This philosophy guides every aspect of our conference planning.
